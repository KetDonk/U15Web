About BPS

Who are we?

The BPS Division at Burnley College is a premier learning community that provides world-class education in Business, Travel and Tourism, Uniformed Services, and Computing. With a focus on innovation, excellence, and real-world experience, the BPS Division offers a range of courses that are designed to prepare students for successful careers in their chosen fields. Our expert faculty members are experienced professionals who bring their real-world expertise into the classroom, and our state-of-the-art facilities provide students with the tools and resources they need to succeed. Whether you are looking to start a new career, advance in your current job, or explore a new field, the BPS Division has the courses and support services you need to achieve your goals. We are committed to providing a dynamic and engaging learning experience that prepares students for success in a rapidly changing world. Join us at the BPS Division and unlock your potential today!
