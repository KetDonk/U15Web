Extra Curricular

A Key Part of College Life: Extra-Curricular Activities

Joining Burnley College is not just about studying, it's also about expanding your interests and discovering new hobbies. Our extra-curricular activities programme is designed to ensure you can make new friends, broaden your horizons, and explore your favourite pastimes. This programme is available to students on A Level and Vocational study programmes, as well as UCB undergraduates and Themis Apprentices.

Freshers' Fair

Don't miss out on our annual Freshers' Fair, which is a fun-filled day for new students to take advantage of offers from local businesses, enjoy free food, and learn about the clubs, societies, and student roles that make up college life. You'll have the opportunity to try new activities and meet new friends. We also stage a Refreshers' Fair later in the academic year to showcase additional opportunities for personal growth.

Travel Opportunities

As a Burnley College student, you'll have the chance to travel the world and take part in planned activities and visits that enhance your studies and help you discover new cultures. Previous trips have included volunteering on conservation projects and teaching English in Thailand, visiting the E3 gaming convention in Los Angeles, and exploring American politics and culture in Washington and New York.

Atrium Activities

Our Student Services team works hard to bring you exciting experiences during breaks from classes. The Atrium is a hub of activity with regular events such as VR experiences, E-Sports, seasonal activities, archery, live music, film screenings, board games, and funfair attractions.

Clubs and Societies

Burnley College offers a range of clubs and societies run by tutors, support staff, and students. Whether you're interested in French, Raspberry Pi, debating, photography, gaming, creative writing, radio, Duke of Edinburgh, or chess, there's something for everyone. Most clubs are held during lunch breaks or after classes, giving you the chance to immerse yourself in your favourite hobby and meet others who share your interests.
