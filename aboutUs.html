About us

Who are we?

Burnley College is a vibrant and dynamic learning community that offers a wide range of courses to students of all ages and backgrounds. With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced and dedicated staff, and a commitment to excellence in education, Burnley College is the perfect place to start or further your academic journey. Whether you are looking to gain new skills, progress in your career, or pursue a new passion, we have a course that will suit your needs. Our courses are designed to be flexible, with full-time and part-time options available, as well as evening and weekend classes. We also offer a range of support services to help you succeed, including tutoring, career advice, and financial aid. At Burnley College, we believe that education should be accessible to everyone, and we are committed to helping you achieve your goals. Staff team About BPS
