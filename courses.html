Burnley College Available Courses

Courses

BTEC Uniformed Services

This course provides students with a comprehensive introduction to the uniformed services industry, including roles and responsibilities, organizational structure, and key skills and knowledge. Topics covered may include leadership and teamwork, communication, law and ethics, and health and safety.

T Level Travel and Tourism

This T Level provides students with an in-depth understanding of the travel and tourism industry, including customer service, marketing and promotion, and tour operations. Students will develop a range of practical skills, including travel and tourism product knowledge, sales and marketing, and event planning and management.

BTEC lvl 2 Exploring Tourism, Hospitality and Events

This course provides students with an introduction to the exciting world of tourism, hospitality, and events. Students will learn about the different aspects of these industries, including customer service, event planning, and the impact of tourism on local communities. They will also gain practical skills in areas such as food preparation and service, housekeeping, and front office operations.

BTEC lvl 1 Travel and Tourism

This course provides students with a basic understanding of the travel and tourism industry, including key destinations, types of holidays, and the different roles within the industry. Students will develop practical skills in areas such as customer service, sales and marketing, and event planning and management.

BTEC lvl 1 Business

This course provides students with an introduction to the fundamentals of business, including finance, marketing, and operations management. Students will develop basic skills in areas such as entrepreneurship, leadership, and teamwork, and gain an understanding of the ethical and legal issues facing businesses today.

BTEC lvl 2 Business and Marketing

This course provides students with a comprehensive understanding of the principles and practices of business and marketing. Students will learn about the different aspects of marketing, including market research, branding, and advertising, and will develop practical skills in areas such as market analysis, product development, and sales and marketing.
